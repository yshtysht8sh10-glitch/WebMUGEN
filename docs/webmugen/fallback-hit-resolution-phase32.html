Fallback Hit Resolution Phase32

This phase adds fallback hit resolution using AIR Clsn1 and Clsn2 boxes.

  • Attack boxes are taken from current AIR animation Clsn1
  • Body boxes are taken from current AIR animation Clsn2
  • Fallback attack applies damage and hit pause
  • Defender enters fallback hit state 5000
  • Attacker marks hitDefUsed to prevent repeated hits
